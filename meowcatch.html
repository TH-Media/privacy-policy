Privacy Policy for Meow Catch – Games for Cats

Last updated: 2025-09-03

Meow Catch – Games for Cats (the “App”) is an entertainment app. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how information may be handled when you use the App.

Information We Collect

  • No account or personal details: The App does not create user accounts and does not ask you to provide personal information (such as name, email, phone number, or address).
  • Advertising identifiers & interactions: The App uses third-party advertising services (Google AdMob). These services may collect device information such as the Advertising ID and basic interaction events (e.g., ad impressions/clicks) to deliver and measure ads.

Third-Party Services

We may show ads via Google AdMob. Data collected by AdMob is processed under Google’s own policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • AdMob & Google Mobile Ads: AdMob policy & data

We do not control how these third parties use the data they collect. Please review their policies for details.

How We Use and Share Data

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • Advertising identifiers and interaction signals may be shared with AdMob/Google to display, personalize, and measure ads.

Data Security & Retention

Network communications made by the App and AdMob use modern encryption in transit (e.g., HTTPS). The App itself does not store personal data. Any retention performed by third-party providers follows their own policies.

Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected personal data from a child, please contact us and we will address the issue promptly.

Your Choices

  • Reset/Limit Ads Personalization (Android): On your device go to Settings → Google → Ads (or Privacy → Ads), then reset the Advertising ID or turn off ad personalization.
  • If the App presents a consent dialog (Google UMP), you can manage your ad consent there.
